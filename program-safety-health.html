Safety & Health Programs

1. The Role of Execution Engineers in Safety Implementation

Objectives:
  • Provide engineers with knowledge of engineering supervision procedures in accordance with the laws and regulations governing quality and implementing them on projects.
  • Understand the concept of professional occupational safety and health and the implementation of its concepts on projects.
  • Familiarize themselves with the tasks and responsibilities of a safety engineer and occupational safety and health procedures according to the requirements of the Egyptian labor law and the requirements of international standards.
  • Understand the occupational safety and health requirements on projects according to Egyptian Labor Law No. 12 of 2003.
Main Axes:
  • The role of the consultant office and engineering supervision in the responsibility of implementing safety
  • Laws regulating engineering supervision
  • The role of supervising engineers in the responsibility of implementing safety on projects
  • Laws governing supervision engineers
  • Responsibility towards implementing safety and safety instructions on projects
  • Risk analysis in engineering projects - risk assessment in engineering projects - the requirements of the Egyptian labor law for safety implementation on the project (employers, engineers, consultants, workers)
  • Methods of project analysis and measurement in planning for safety on the project - accident and injury assessment method
  • Hazard analysis and risk assessment in engineering projects - occupational safety and health requirements in engineering projects
  • Safety requirements for each trade during construction, use of materials, and tools in construction projects
  • Occupational safety and health plans for construction projects

2. Occupational Safety and Health

Objectives:
  • Improve and enhance safety knowledge for engineers and technicians working in the field or technical office
  • Understand occupational safety and health application in industrial, professional, and construction facilities
  • Familiarize participants with international standards (ISO-45001-2018) in occupational safety and health management systems
  • Provide all legally required professional safety and health requirements in construction projects and industrial facilities
Main Axes:
  • Occupational safety and health requirements according to Egyptian Labor Law No. 12 of 2003
  • Safety and health management systems
  • Identifying, analyzing and assessing risks in engineering facilities according to OHSAS 18001 Occupational Safety & Health and Egyptian labor law standards
  • Accident and injury reporting system
  • Root cause analysis of accidents and corrective & preventive actions
  • Safety and health planning in engineering projects and industrial facilities
  • Work permits system - safety and health hazards in engineering projects (excavations, formwork, work at heights, confined spaces)
  • Fundamentals of fire detection and firefighting - types of fires - fire warning systems and firefighting equipment
  • Occupational hazards and diseases due to chemical, physical and biological factors
  • Occupational health and medical surveillance
  • Requirements for safety and occupational health in engineering workshops and stores
  • Personal protective equipment - types and fields of use
  • Emergency planning and crisis management
Enroll in This Program

3. Certified Occupational Safety and Health Supervisor

Objectives:
  • Understand the occupational safety and health application system in industrial, professional, and construction facilities.
  • Knowledge of accidents, occupational hazards and how to evaluate risks and take preventive measures to avoid accidents.
  • Prepare security plans and evaluate them.
  • Understand the requirements and standards of the Egyptian labor law and international occupational safety and health standards.
  • Integrate a safe management system throughout the project phases (planning and design, implementation, operation and exit).
Main Axes:
  • The occupational health and safety management system
  • Basics of Egyptian labor law and occupational safety requirements
  • Introduction to ISO 45001:2018 international standards for occupational health and safety management systems
  • Identifying the elements of the risks and hazards of occupational safety and health
  • Risk assessment and analysis
  • Requirements for occupational safety and health in engineering construction projects and the role of supervision in implementing them
  • Work permits system in construction projects
  • Safety and health hazards during different construction stages (excavations, formwork, work at heights, confined spaces)
  • Firefighting fundamentals (fires and classification - fire protection systems - planning for dealing with fire emergencies)
  • Emergency planning and crisis management
  • Accident reporting, investigation and analysis
  • Personal protective equipment - types and methods of use
  • Field applications and case studies
Enroll in This Program

4. Securing Work at Heights

Objectives:
  • Familiarize engineers and technicians with all risks they may encounter while working at heights on projects and the appropriate means for maintaining their safety.
  • Enable participants to identify the requirements for each tool and equipment used in work at heights.
  • Provide all health and safety requirements during work at heights, starting with how to perform safe work and carry out inspection of equipment.
  • Identify how to prepare and evaluate work at heights plans to ensure workers' safety.
Main Axes:
  • Introduction to occupational safety and health and the concepts of work at heights
  • Definition of work at heights - work at heights according to the Egyptian labor law
  • Work at heights permit system
  • Requirements for work at heights safety and the equipment needed for implementing it
  • Methods of implementing safe work at heights (scaffolding - external ladders - suspended scaffolds - lifting and access equipment - mechanical equipment - cranes - work baskets)
  • Personal protective equipment for work at heights
  • Fall risks and how to prevent them
  • Securing work at heights with fall arrest, restraint, or positioning systems
  • Daily, periodic and detailed inspection procedures for personal protective equipment for work at heights
  • Safe practices when using equipment and during work at heights
  • Case studies and practical applications
Enroll in This Program

5. Insulation and Protection of Concrete Structures

Objectives:
  • Familiarize participants with sources of corrosion in concrete and their effects.
  • Learn how to treat cracks, strengthening and reinforcing concrete structures.
  • Understand the latest technologies and materials used in insulation and concrete protection.
  • Familiarize with the applications of heat and sound insulation.
  • Learn about waterproofing and moisture insulation in foundations and roofs.
  • Understand the quality of insulation implementation and necessary tests.
Main Axes:
  • Review of concrete structures and all their components with theoretical engineering foundations and concepts.
  • Sources of concrete corrosion and their effects on concrete structures.
  • Methods and types of waterproofing insulation (foundations - roofs).
  • Methods and types of thermal and sound insulation.
  • Crack treatment and strengthening of concrete structures.
  • Introduction to additives and materials, their types and uses.
  • Materials, their types and methods of mixing and application (Epoxy - Polyurethane - Bitumen - Acrylic and their derivatives).
  • Review of some case studies (construction stages, materials and applied thickness, and quality assessment methods).
