Program Overview

Our Procurement & Supply Chain Programs are designed to develop professionals who can optimize supply chain operations, manage vendor relationships, and implement strategic sourcing practices. Learn to reduce costs, improve efficiency, and drive value across the entire supply chain.

Duration

11 weeks comprehensive program
33 hours total training

Certification

CIPS Preparation Certificate
Professional Recognition

What You'll Learn

Strategic Procurement Planning
Supplier Relationship Management
Contract Negotiation & Management
Inventory Management Systems
Logistics & Distribution Management
Supply Chain Risk Management
Cost Analysis & Optimization
Technology in Supply Chain

Program Modules

Introduction to procurement principles, sourcing strategies, and supplier evaluation techniques for effective purchasing decisions.

Advanced supply chain design, logistics planning, and performance measurement to optimize operational efficiency and reduce costs.

Building strategic partnerships, contract negotiation techniques, and vendor performance management for sustainable relationships.
