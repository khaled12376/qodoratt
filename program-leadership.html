Leadership Programs

1. Leadership and Creative Skills and Effective Leadership

Program Objectives:
  • Enhance leadership skills in activating creative and innovative thinking strategies in the organization
  • Excel in creative thinking and analytical thinking methodologies to formulate a balance in human mental powers (Mind Mapping)
  • Identify different innovative thinking tools and practice them practically in solving and formulating problems
  • Study types of administrative excellence and their functional relationship with concepts of administrative creativity
  • Know modern administrative, innovative, and creative skills in problem-solving and decision-making
  • Know the characteristics of the contemporary administrative environment and the necessity of excellence by activating creative and innovative capabilities in the organization
Main Axes:
  • Administrative creativity and innovative thinking tools and their impact on entrepreneurship
  • Fundamentals of creative and innovative thinking and its application in work areas
  • Enhancing the creative and innovative environment in the work
  • Creative and innovative thinking methods and how to apply them and involve those responsible for implementation
  • The necessity of excellence and the characteristics of the contemporary administrative environment
  • Methods to overcome obstacles facing the application of new methods and how to deal with them to achieve distinguished administrative performance

2. Situational Leadership

Program Objectives:
  • Provide participants with the necessary knowledge of the recognized leadership styles
  • Provide participants with the necessary knowledge of the requirements for situational leadership
  • Provide participants with the necessary skills to deal with permanent changes from the perspective of situational leadership
  • Familiarize participants with different experiences within the framework of reviewing contemporary leadership models
Main Axes:
  • Patterns of leadership models
  • Qualities, knowledge, and skills required for situational leadership
  • Situational leadership and its administrative requirements
  • Situational leadership skills
  • Situational leadership test
  • Case studies for analyzing situational leadership
Enroll in This Program

3. Developing, Motivating, and Leading High-Performance Teams

Program Objectives:
  • Understand group dynamics
  • Create the environment for high performance
  • Methods, techniques, and practices for preparing and building a high-performance team
  • How to develop team spirit
  • Team development stages: accelerating the process
  • Set clearly defined goals for the team and develop an action plan to achieve them
  • Define clear roles and responsibilities within the team
Main Axes:
  • Effective management and leadership skills and the leadership personality
  • Building a high-performance team
  • Methodologies for leading individuals in the work system
  • Leadership and strategic decision-making
  • Leadership strategies and performance quality
  • Competencies of excellence in leading work teams
  • Leadership... and mechanisms for developing teamwork
Enroll in This Program

4. Smart Leadership and Creative Competencies in Work

Program Objectives:
  • Build an overview of the smart leadership system and its main roles
  • Learn how to build teams capable of self-organization for projects that require flexibility
  • Enhance good leadership practices in situations of uncertainty, complexity, and turbulence
  • Introduce the concepts and scientific foundations for administrative excellence and innovation
  • Know smart leadership methods and the power of decision-making
  • Employ innovative thinking in problem-solving and making high-quality decisions
  • Think strategically, compete successfully, and seize growth opportunities
  • Lead authentically and ethically at all levels and foster a culture of accountability throughout the organization
Main Axes:
  • Fundamentals of smart leadership
  • Smart and creative leadership strategies
  • Characteristics of creative leaders
  • Modern administrative and leadership strategies
  • Concept of innovation and creativity
