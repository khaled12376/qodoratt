Program Overview

This advanced phase completes the qualification process by introducing specialized and complex management topics. It is designed to prepare engineers for leadership roles by covering crucial areas like Value Engineering, the intricacies of Contracts and Claims Management, and Project Risk Management. The phase also integrates advanced leadership skills.

Key Topics Covered

  • Value Engineering Principles and Applications
  • Contracts and Claims Management
  • Project Risk Management
  • Advanced Leadership Skills for Engineers
  • Strategic Project Planning
  • Cost-Benefit Analysis
  • Negotiation and Conflict Resolution
  • Change Order Management

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of Phase Three, participants will be able to:

  • Apply value engineering techniques to optimize project value
  • Manage complex contracts and handle claims effectively
  • Identify, assess, and mitigate project risks
  • Demonstrate advanced leadership capabilities in engineering projects
  • Make strategic decisions on project direction and changes
  • Lead multidisciplinary engineering teams
