About Us

We Are Quodrat

Quodrat is the training and development arm of Egypt’s Ministry of Public Enterprises. Backed by the Holding Company for Construction and Development, we specialize in building human capital, enhancing competitiveness, and delivering impactful, industry-aligned training programs.

With certified trainers, world-class facilities, and partnerships across global institutions, we empower professionals and organizations to unlock their full potential and thrive in a fast-changing world.
