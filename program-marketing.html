Program Overview

Our Marketing Programs provide comprehensive training in marketing strategies, digital marketing, brand management, customer engagement, market research, and promotional campaigns. Master marketing planning, social media marketing, content strategy, consumer behavior analysis, and build effective marketing strategies that drive sales and create lasting customer relationships. Our curriculum covers 4 specialized programs designed to develop marketing professionals at all levels, from marketing coordinators to strategic marketing managers.

Our 4 Specialized Marketing Programs

1. Marketing Strategies and Plans

About the Program:
You will be able to build marketing plans and strategies that lead to increased organizational sales and gain customer loyalty by meeting their needs and retaining them.

Program Objectives:
  • Acquire new customers and persuade them to buy the product or service, which leads to increased profitability of the organization.
  • Plan and develop products or services desired and required by the target market.
  • Promote products and services, aiming to inform and convince customers about the goods and satisfy their needs and desires.
  • Define various pricing, distribution, and promotion methods that reflect value and benefit for all.
  • Provide technical and non-technical services before and after the sale process.
  • Keep pace with continuous development and improvement and achieve the highest levels of competition in local and international markets.
  • Develop the organization's market share in international markets.
Enroll in This Program

2. Digital Marketing

About the Program:
You will be able to perform planning and follow-up tasks for marketing the organization's products and services through Internet channels and electronic applications. You will be able to provide customer care through social media.

Program Objectives:
  • Market services and products online.
  • Plan the design of electronic commercial websites.
  • Prepare and implement e-marketing strategies.
  • Prepare and implement e-marketing tactics.
  • Promotion and advertising via the Internet.
Enroll in This Program

3. Customer Relationship and Experience Management (CRM/CEM)

About the Program:
You will be able to perform the planning functions to build and apply a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system to increase the organization's profitability and market share by increasing customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Program Objectives:
  • Apply Customer Relationship Management techniques.
  • Manage Customer Experiences.
  • Provide distinguished service to customers.
  • Deal with customer complaints and suggestions.
  • Plan Customer Relationship and Experience Management.
Enroll in This Program

4. Skills for Customer Retention and Care

About the Program:
You will be able to plan and organize to provide the best practices in customer care and retention, and offer them distinguished service. You will gain skills in effective communication with customers and positive dealing with customer complaints. You will also be able to deal professionally with the behavioral patterns of different customer personalities. You will gain the ability to measure customer loyalty to confirm the provision of customer care and retention.

Program Objectives:
  • Develop customer care programs and work on customer retention.
  • Measure customer satisfaction and loyalty in a feasible and systematic way for care and retention.
  • Work according to strategies and tactics related to customer retention and care.
  • Deal with different behavioral patterns of customers.
  • Deal with customer complaints and suggestions for care and retention.
  • Effectively and distinguishably retain and care for customers to achieve their satisfaction and gain their loyalty.
  • Apply strategies and best practices for customer retention and care.
Enroll in This Program
