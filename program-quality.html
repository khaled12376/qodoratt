Program Overview

Our Quality Programs provide comprehensive training in quality management systems, ISO standards, quality assurance, process improvement, and excellence frameworks. Master quality control, Six Sigma methodologies, lean management, audit procedures, and build effective quality strategies that drive organizational excellence and continuous improvement. Our curriculum covers 11 specialized programs designed to develop quality professionals at all levels, from quality coordinators to ISO auditors and excellence specialists.

1. Analysis and Simplification of Work Procedures

About the Program:
Identify the best international practicesIdentify the best international practices that will help you understand the causes of complexity, identify areas for improvement by focusing on eliminating the causes of wasted human effort, simplifying and standardizing procedures, and automating opportunities. Gain skills in using tools for drawing process and procedure flowcharts, detecting problems and opportunities for improvement, and developing essential knowledge and skills in simplifying organizational operations and procedures and work analysis.

Program Objectives:
  • Identify the principles of organizational design and its relation to the complexity of work procedures.
  • Analyze the most important principles and steps in procedure simplification.
  • Use workflow maps, organizational design maps, and work distribution maps.
  • Perform simplification of complex procedures as part of a practical study.

3. Quality Assurance

About the Program:
You will develop the knowledge and skills for the Quality Assurance function, which will enable you to follow a systematic preventive approach to ensure customer needs and requirements are met and managed correctly. Ensure that internal processes in the organization are designed to achieve its goals and objectives. You will be able to plan and implement an internal audit program that helps you track responsibilities and corrective actions taken.

Program Objectives:
  • Identify the concept of Quality Assurance, the quality management system, and the essential components and prerequisites of a quality management system.
  • Know the relationship between Quality Assurance management and other departments.
  • Communicate with customers and determine their requirements.
  • Manage and document administrative processes.
  • Prepare the Quality Policy in the company.
  • Conduct internal review processes for the system.
Enroll in This Program

4. Fundamentals of Six Sigma

About the Program:
You will learn a new methodology that may save your company millions of dollars. The Six Sigma methodology is an application focused on achieving significant financial results and increasing customer satisfaction. When employed accurately on selected projects, this methodology leads to a significant reduction or control of inefficient operations and identifying and eliminating the causes of defects and errors, leading to major professional gains for organizations.

Program Objectives:
  • Develop and plan the application of the Six Sigma methodology.
  • Apply the principles of the Six Sigma performance improvement model (DMAIC).
  • Study the different elements for executing an integrated improvement project.
  • Establish statistical hypotheses using simple statistical tools.
  • Use qualitative tools to solve problems related to improvement projects.
  • Reduce errors and defects by applying Lean and Six Sigma.
  • Collect, analyze, and quantitatively estimate data that helps improve processes.
  • Employ statistical analysis using statistical programs and tools.
Enroll in This Program

5. Total Quality Management

About the Program:
Develop your knowledge and skills to enable you to apply the principles of Total Quality Management (TQM) efficiently and effectively.

Program Objectives:
  • Identify the different quality concepts and quality management, and the elements upon which the TQM system is based.
  • Know the eight principles in TQM.
  • Apply the process approach in TQM, understand the relationships between processes, and improve work procedures.
  • Communicate with customers, determine their requirements, and utilize TQM principles to improve customer satisfaction.
  • Realize the importance of motivating and involving employees under TQM.
  • Select and use appropriate quality and continuous improvement tools to enhance the performance of the organization and its internal operations.
  • Advise and influence management to adopt the TQM philosophy as a strategic approach for the organization.
Enroll in This Program

  • Define productivity and the different types of waste in the organization.

  • Differentiate and apply the measurement of initiatives and set goals to improve productivity.

11. ISO 9001:2015 Specification

About the Program:
You will be able to know and understand the standards and fundamentals upon which the ISO 9001 specification is based. You will learn the requirements for obtaining the ISO 9001:2015 certificate, which relates to having a quality management system in the organization that ensures customer satisfaction. You will also develop the essential knowledge that enables you to maintain the internal Quality Management System in the organization to mimic the clauses of the specification.

Program Objectives:
  • Identify the ISO 9000 family of specifications and its branches.
  • Understand the requirements of the ISO 9001:2015 standard and the advantages of applying the management system.
  • Prepare a risk-based Quality Management System.
  • Understand the auditing mechanism and the use of special forms in the internal review.
  • Follow a special mechanism for following up on corrective actions resulting from non-conformity cases.
