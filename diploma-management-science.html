Postgraduate Diploma in Management Science

About the Diploma

This diploma offers an in-depth study of five main management areas through an efficient and flexible schedule. It is a standalone diploma or can serve as a gateway for further academic pursuits. The diploma aims to provide participants with theoretical study and practical management skills through exposure to a variety of market and management problems. Participants will apply applicable academic theories and principles, and will be able to provide value to both employers and themselves on a regular basis through the program. Since change is a characteristic of the current era, the program aims to update and develop concepts and the critical application of qualitative and quantitative issues specialized in management science. It is directed at those who have relevant practical experience and hold mid-to-high management positions. The program will enable them to develop applied skills and competencies, which will provide opportunities for intellectual growth and help management professionals develop a sound business understanding and prepare them to acquire foundational competencies in a variety of management fields to improve their managerial decision-making abilities.

Key Topics (Modules)

  • Principles of Management.
  • Communication Skills.
  • Human Resource Management.
  • Marketing and Sales Management.
  • Financial Fundamentals.
  • Contemporary Management Practices.
  • Decision Making and Business Ethics.
