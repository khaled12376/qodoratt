Postgraduate Diploma in Accounting and Finance

About the Diploma

The Comprehensive Accounting Diploma is aimed at all professionals in the field of management and financial accounting, starting from accounts trainees up to department managers and owners of medium and large enterprises. The diploma helps its students not only to break into the accounting field and understand its terms and basic principles, but also moves with them step-by-step, ensuring they eventually reach a level of accounting professionalism. The Accounting Diploma covers in detail both accounting and financial analysis, how to handle and categorize inventory adjustments, the purpose of financial statements and the mechanism for dealing with them, in addition to the fields of tax, real estate, commercial, and industrial accounting, among others.

Key Topics

  • The theoretical framework of accounting.
  • Recording financial transactions.
  • Branches of accounting.
  • How to analyze financial transactions using the balance sheet equation.
  • How to determine the transactions that must be recorded in the accounting records.
  • Preparing the trial balance.
  • The method for preparing the statement of cash flows.
  • Understanding the effect of adjustments on financial statements.
  • The accounting treatment for end-of-service benefits.
